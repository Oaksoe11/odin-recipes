Chicken Curry Burmese Style

This is a traditional burmese cuisine which has served a dozen generations of hundreds of ethnic groups in Myanmar.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • chilli
  • tumeric powder
  • peperika
  • chicken

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite sized cubes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with peprika, tumeric powder, salt and chicken bouillion.
  3. Fry onions, garlic and ginger using cooking oil till they are thoroughly crushed.
  4. Add the marinated chicken and let it cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add some water and cover with a lid.
  6. Let is cook for 20-30 mins.
  7. Enjoy!!
